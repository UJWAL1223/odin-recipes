Garlic Mushroom

These garlic mushrooms are easy to make by saut√©ing sliced mushrooms in butter with red wine. Delicious with grilled steaks or on top of toasted bread for a tasty appetizer

For this recipe it will take about 5 minutes for preparation and 15 minutes for cooking total 20 minutes and can be searved to 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 pounds sliced fresh mushroom
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 cup red wine

Steps:

  • Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.Add mushrooms and garlic; cook and stir untill mushrooms are a light golden brown and liquid has evaporated, about 10 minutes.Stir in basil
  • Reduce heat to low, and pour wine into skillet. Simmer untill wine has mostly evaporated. Serve immediately
